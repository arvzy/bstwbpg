About BESTIE


Introducing BESTIE, The Basic Electronic Stationary Terminal for Indoor Exploration This application software aims to guide the users (students, school personnel, visitors, etc.) to their destination whilst being stationary.

Features of BESTIE


BESTIE is actually an acronym. It stands for Basic Electronic Stationary Terminal for Indoor Environment.

Basically, it is a wayfinding application that is used inside the Academic Center of STI College Fairview

We wanted to make it available to all buildings of STI, but due to our limited time and capabilities,

we decided to create a BESTIE for the Academic Center only.
